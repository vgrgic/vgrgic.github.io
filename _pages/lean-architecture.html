Requirements and strategy is changing constantly. Agile embraces, and even enables faster change. Architecture must keep up with this change. By applying lean principles in enterprise and solution architecture, you will still maintain cohesive IT landscape, and sustainable speed.

LeanArch provides custom training: Lean / Agile Architecture and coaching for architects, analysts and developers.
